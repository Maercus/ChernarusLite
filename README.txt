Chernarus Lite is a mod project developed by Asmondian, MaercusTargaryen, and the development team of the "Day Zero" server. Both its mappings and dynamic events, as well as its files, are open-source, and anyone can use them on any platform you wish. However, it is prohibited to claim credit or authorship for any of these.

This project aims to offer a dynamic, innovative, and functional alternative to the immensity of Chernarus, rescuing the virtues of smaller maps and complementing them with new (vanilla) elements. In other words, it involves a reduced, dynamic, and redesigned version of Chernarus with new and improved points of interest, quality of life modifications, and a post-apocalyptic atmosphere.

Currently, it is compatible with PC servers, and we are working on adapting it for consoles (PS/XBOX).

For more information, visit our website at https://chernaruslite.com/ or join our Discord at https://discord.com/invite/RC6HxaK2rd.

INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS

-Copy the "dayzLite.chernarusplus" folder and paste it into the "mpmissions" folder on your server.
-In your serverDZ.cfg file, replace // template="dayzOffline.chernarusplus" // with // template="dayzLite.chernarusplus" //
If your server is for PC, you can load the mappings in two ways. If it is for consoles, you can only use method B:

a) Use the shared .dze file in the EditorFiles folder, loading the "DayZ Editor Loader" mod.

b) Load the .json file(s) (still in development) located in the "custom" folder through the cfggameplay.json file.
DO NOT USE BOTH METHODS SIMULTANEOUSLY.
If you followed the steps correctly, the map should now be installed.
